About

Quality as a product instinct, not a department.

I'm a Senior Business Analyst and Product thinker at Shell, working at the intersection of e-mobility platforms and delivery excellence. My background is in Quality Engineering at the Principal and Architect level — which means I've spent years asking the question every good PM forgets to ask: what breaks this, and why?

I'm pursuing an MBA in Product Leadership from the Institute of Product Leadership, and I write publicly about platform strategy, systems thinking, and the craft of product decisions. My work lives at the crossroads of rigour and imagination.
